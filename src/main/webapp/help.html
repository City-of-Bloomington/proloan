Help Desk

Please fill in the Help Desk Request form.

Note: the only required information is the Problem/Request Details.
  • Make sure your Username, Department and BC listed in the form are correct. If not contact the ITS Help Desk at 349-3454.
  • The PC Inventeory Number is the number on the front of your computer (not the monitor).
  • Please select the most appropriate Category and Item from the drop down lists. If none of the selections seems appropriate, just leave Category and/or Item blank.
  • Type a short description in the Problem/Request Title line.
  • Type in a detailed description in the Problem/Request Details. Be as specific and clear as possible.
  • When you click on Submit, an email will be sent to ITS with your request, and another email will be sent to you with a ticket request number so that you can lool up information on the status of your request.
  • ITS may contact you for further information on the details of your request.
